Emotional Intelligence

What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional Intelligence is related to things like drive, empathy and leadership. Having high emotional intelligence means you are able to relate with people on a really good level and be able to hear and understand people's problems in way that means you might be able to help them.

How is it different to IQ?

Emotional Intelligence is being able to read and understand people as well as working well in teams and helping people overcome their challenges. IQ is physical intelligence like how quickly you can understand concepts or memorize syntax.

Why is it important?

EQ is very important when working with teams of people as you need to be able to listen to people's problems and feedback and understand exactly what they are trying to communicate. It's important for learning how to be a good listener and for giving valuable feedback of your own. It controls the ability to be tactful which is putting things in such a way that you get your point across without hurting peoples's feelings.
